About Us

SAMCO Painting & Decorating specializes in professionally managing and executing small to big Painting Projects across the area; starting from Exterior & Interior Painting, Cabinets Refinishing, Fences Stain/Painting, Color Matching and Consultation, Painting Touchups, Wallpaper Installation & Removal, Texture, Caulking, Pressure Wash as well as Rain Gutter Cleaning.

Having our OSHA 30-Construction taught us how important is to follow the right Health & Safety procedures and measurements are, for the safety of our crew as well as our clients.

With Quality, Reliability and Honesty as our main values, we are proudly saying that we are ahead of the market by our client satisfaction approach. Our clients are our partners in success, if we fail any of our clients we fail our vision.
